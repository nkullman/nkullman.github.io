The Fonz had better speed up

Since calculating The Fonz's minimum launch speed to clear the record 14 garbage cans on his motorcycle, I've been fascinated by our ability to make predictions about nature through mathematics.
Besides the work of my fellow QERMies, some of my favorite applications include the Kepler mission's search for Earth-like planets and the idea of colonizing Venus.
